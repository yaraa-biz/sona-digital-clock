About Dr. A. Mahendran

Dr. A. Mahendran is a distinguished academician with expertise in Information Technology, Computer Science, Statistics, and Applied Data Science. His academic credentials include M.Sc., NET qualification, Ph.D., and CCNA certification, making him a well-rounded professional in the field of technology education.

On October 16, 2024, he was awarded a doctorate degree by the Honorable Governor of Tamil Nadu, Mr. R. N. Ravi, marking a significant milestone in his academic career.
